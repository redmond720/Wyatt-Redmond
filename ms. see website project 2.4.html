About Delta High School

Delta High is a Stem Based high school, highly focusing on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. However we also keep art and literature a big part of delta. For example, instead of having an art class you will learn graphic design and draft and design, or instead of having just a english class you will have the class, media literacy.

Delta is all about collaboration and project base learning; which mean that most of your work will be a project and being able to work with others to get the project done. This style of learning allows you or your child learn real world skill and simulates a real work place environment.
